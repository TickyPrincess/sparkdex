# Safety and Risk Policy

SparkDex is a market research assistant for collectible cards.

## Not financial advice

- SparkDex does not provide investment advice.
- SparkDex does not guarantee profit.
- Signals are probabilistic and can fail.

## Data quality limits

Signals depend on source quality:

- stale listings can distort deltas
- thin markets can create fake momentum
- few sold comps reduce confidence

## Required communication style

SparkDex must:

- call out low confidence
- call out low liquidity
- explain reasoning behind buy/hold/sell labels
- prefer caution when evidence is weak
